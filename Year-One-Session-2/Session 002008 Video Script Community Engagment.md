Session 002008 Video Script Community Engagment

It is Current Events and Community Engagement time.  This is where we work on the practicalities of our Champions work.  

First of all, take some time as a group to list some CURRENT EVENTS that might be appropriate for you to discuss.  Are there any agenda items of interest that your local school board are discussing?  List those.  If this your State Legislature is in session, are there any BILLS that affect religious liberty causes in any way - are they being discussed at the Capitol?  Should your group discuss these or research these topics?  Of course, is there legislation regarding religious liberty causes being worked on in Washington D.C.?  Should your group discuss or plan to become better informed? 

One of the things your Champions Chapter might want to do is locate some of the Information Resources that are readily available.  I have a number of them that I consult with every week, that keep in the KNOW.  I will be suggesting some of them to you for your consideration.  One of the sources is ADF or Aliance Defending Freedom, the legal defense group.  You can go to their website and research the cases that they are defending and updating frequently.  

There are websites that you can find that help you to see what the Legislatures are considering, discussing, and finally deciding.  Interestingly, you if didn’t know before, but some of the Bills take months and even years to finally make it to a vote on the floor.  Many of them don’t make it to the floor for a decision.  Some of them get sandwiched into other bills.  

Wouldn’t it be great if we developed together a watch-dog-type of group that could sift through all of the information, stay updated on a bill’s whereabouts, and then sound the alarm well enough in advance if a religious liberty issue is suddenly jeapardy.  Sometimes we discover things too late because we didn’t stay alert.  

Here in Session 2, what I want you to be doing is listing some of the possibilities of what your group could dig into.  There will be work to do in the future.  Some of you might be willing to go to the school board meetings, take turns going to your local school board meetings.  As always, we go first to listen and learn.  Secondly, we discover who and what we as a Christian group can be praying for.  When it comes time to advocate for something or someone, we will do that, but only after we’ve taken plenty of time to listen, to learn, to understand and to pray.
