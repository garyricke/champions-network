God Works Two Distinct Ways

Hi!  I am Pastor Mark Frith, Director of the Champions Netowrk for the Lutheran Center for Religious Liberty.  Lesson number one is to know that this is our first acronyn.  Lutheran Center for Religious Liberty is otherwise known as L C R L.  In general acronyms are not very helpful.  In a congregation, arcroymns work for all time members, but for any new, you create a learning barrier.  It took me 1.5 years to learn all the acronyms that we use in this office.  

Mom and Pop Papers are meant to be simply written and help teach the basic concepts of religious liberty and our Champions Network.  You can see, the first one is short.  Hopefully you have a copy of Mom and Pop #1 in front of you.  You will talk about it after this video. 

You can read this on your own. In fact I want you to read this several times over the next month. Digest it.  In your round table session group like right now, if there is something you don’t understand ask one another.  Then as you digest, see if you tell your spouse or a friend this concept…and do so without looking at your sheet.  That would be the goal.  We want this to be specific and short so that you can do just that. You are slowly being equipped.

The simplicity of Mom and Pop One is in the title:  God Works two distinct ways in our world.  Sure it could be expounded up and made complex, but you’re used to breaking things down into bite size understandings. 

Let me give you an example.  What do you really need to know about owning a car?  Well, two things:  How to drive safely, and how to maintain it properly.  Or there more details about a car, well sure, but those are the two main things. 

God Works in our world these two basic ways: He preserves the world as we know it.  He saves people who trust in Him through Jesus Christ.  

God preserves and God saves.  He uses people to do that for him in so many ways.  

God uses the Church to deliver the saving news and sacraments that save. 

God uses the State or Government to preserve or keep the peace. 

One helps you just for the temporary world.  One helps you to live forever unto all eternity.

The founding of our country and the government that forged by our Founding Fathers, they conceived a gift that no other country had been able to forge.  That makes us different in so many ways.  

Now as a group take some to read the document together, and answer the discussion questions I’ve provided you.  Each of these principles will build on one another equipping you to champion religious liberty for others.  I’m Pastor Mark Frith, thanks for watching this video.
